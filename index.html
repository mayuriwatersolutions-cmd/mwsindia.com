Our Water Softening Process

1

Water Analysis

We begin by testing your water to determine hardness levels and identify specific contaminants.

2

System Selection

Based on the analysis, we recommend the ideal water softening system for your needs.

3

Professional Installation

Our certified technicians install your system with minimal disruption to your home or business.

4

Ion Exchange Process

The system uses resin beads to exchange hardness ions for sodium ions, softening the water.

5

Automatic Regeneration

The system automatically cleans and recharges the resin beads to maintain efficiency.

6

Ongoing Maintenance

We provide regular maintenance and support to keep your system running optimally.
